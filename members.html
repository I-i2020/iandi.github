innovation and inventors

INNOVATIONS AND INVENTORS(I&I) serves as a platform for aspiring people to improve their talent, by making them learn technical skills useful for their career and engage them to work practically in project works to gain professional experience.
Here in I&I, people with innovative ideas are given a team of hard-working members to work with. And all necessary requirements for the project are arranged by the management of I&I, as for it is possible.
people with a desire to learn skills are made to work in those projects so that they learn and work as a team, converting the ideas into a successful invention/innovation.

I&I's kutty story

In our state(Tamil Nadu), most of the parents want their child to become an ENGINEER, as a result, more than 1.5 lakh students are graduating every year. Because of these huge number of graduates, the job gets complicated and the companies expect more marks and skills to filter the crowd.

I&I PROVES AS AN SOLUTION FOR ENGINEER'S JOBLESS SITUATION

Now Consider This Situation, A Male Child from an ordinary middle-class family completes his Higher Secondary Schooling with good marks. He Choses his Engineering Stream and completes his Graduation with Good scores.At an age of 21.Now he Wants a job to fulfill his family’s needs,But Due to overcrowd of Enginnering graduates and less job oppurtunity situation,He is not getting any quality jobs related to his field.Out of his family pressure and finacial needs,He will choose any job,Which his qualification Doesn’t deserve even though he has better knowledge and better skills,He is not getting better jobs as he deserve

THIS IS THE SITUATION OF MOST OF THE ENGINEERS IN TAMIL NADU.

To overcome this crisis, Two Enginnering Students (MR.SAI VISHNU PRASATH AND MR.JERAN XAVIER) Jointly started I&I.
